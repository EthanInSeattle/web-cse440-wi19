Project Overview

Many students and other young people live in apartments with shared laundry resources. This means that they need to coordinate their laundry schedules with other people in the complex. Without explicit coordination, they face issues such as: not knowing when a washer or dryer is available; forgetting to set reminders for when their clothes are finished being washed (and consequently forgetting to move them to the dryer); and not remembering to get the clothing from the dryer when it’s finished. This results in decreased utilization of laundry resources. Our solution involves attaching sensors to each machine so that the user can know when they’re in use; a “laundry ball” that goes in the machine with the user’s clothing, and tracks the laundry status; and an app, called Laundr, to alert the user during different stages of the process (mainly: when machines are available for use; when washing and drying is complete).
